Empowering those with spinal cord injuries and mobility challenges to reclaim independence through resources, community, and unwavering support.

Our Mission is Simple

Meauxbility Empowers

Those battling spinal cord injuries and mobility challenges by providing resources, community, and opportunities that restore independence and create a better quality of life.

Our Vision

A Future Without Limits

Where people of all abilities have equal access to resources, opportunities, and the fullness of life.

More Options + More Access = More Life.
Built by Experience

Driven by Results

A motorcycle accident in 2017 left our founder, Sam Primeaux, paralyzed from the chest down. Through faith, grit, and the support of his community, Sam reclaimed his independence—and turned that journey into a movement.

Empowerment

Obstacles into Opportunity

Today we combine funding, mentorship, and a practical recovery playbook so people with mobility challenges can accelerate their progress toward freedom.

We believe recovery shouldn't depend on wealth—everyone deserves more options, more access, and more life.

Community

The Meauxbility Movement

What began as one story is now a growing network of people and partners creating more access—and more life—together.

No one should face life's hardest challenges alone.

Join us. Belonging, encouragement, and shared strength start here.
