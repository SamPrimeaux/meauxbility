The Gap We're Closing

Three barriers stand between injury and independence. We're removing them.

COST

First-year SCI costs reach $1.4M. Lifetime up to $6.3M. We connect survivors to funding and resources.

KNOWLEDGE

18,400 new SCIs yearly. Families waste precious time searching. We provide instant vetted guidance.

ACCESS

Equipment exists but remains unreachable. We bridge the gap between innovation and those who need it.
